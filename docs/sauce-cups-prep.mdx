## Cheese sauce

- Each can makes 42-44 sauce cups.
- Using an ice-cream scoop, place 2.5 ounces of cheese sauce into 3.5 oz. Souffle cup 
- Cover each cup with a lid and place onto a 1/2 sheet tray, then place in the walk-in cooler.

## Ranch Sauce

- Scoop 3 ounces (weighed) into a souffle cup and cover with a lid.
- Place cups in a 1/3 Lexan
- Label the Lexan
- Place the Lexan into the walk-in

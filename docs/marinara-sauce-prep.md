# Marinara Sauce Prep

![Marinara sauce prep diagram](./images/marinara-sauce-prep.png)

Place the image file in [docs/images/](docs/images) with the filename `marinara-sauce-prep.png` so it renders here.

## Ingredients

- 1 can of Saporito
- 1 can of water
- 1 whole bag of pizza seasoning

## Instructions

1. Combine the Saporito and water.
2. Add the full bag of pizza seasoning.
3. Mix into a 12 qt sauce bucket with a spatula.
4. Do not use the immersion blender.

**Pasta (served with a side of 2 garlic breads)**

**All Pastas Come with Cheese on Top** 

## Pasta Supreme

Marinara Sauce, Sausage, Mushrooms, Onions, Gr. Peppers, Black Olives and Chopped Pepperoni.

## Chicken Alfredo

Alfredo Sauce, Chicken, ½ Cup of Cheese and a Sprinkle of Shaved Parm.

## Spicy Alfredo

Alfredo Sauce, Chopped Pepperoni, Tomatoes, Onions, Jalapenos, ½ Cup of Cheese, and a Sprinkle of Shaved Parm.

## Pasta Buffalo Chicken Ranch

Buffalo and Ranch Sauce, Bacon, Chicken and Onion.

## Pasta Meatball Marinara

Marinara Sauce and (10) Meatballs.

## Pasta Italian Meats

Marinara Sauce, Chopped Pepperoni, Beef, Bacon, Ham, and Sausage.

## Pasta Chicken Parmesan

Marinara Sauce, Chicken Strips(cut up 2 good sized strips and run through oven halfway), ½ cup of Cheese and Shaved Parm

## Pasta Adult Mac and Cheese

1. One ladle of alfredo sauce
2. One cup of nacho cheese sauce
3. **BE SURE TO MIX UP THE SAUCES**
4. ½ CUP OF MOZZARELLA CHEESE
5. ½ CUP OF CHEDDAR CHEESE
6. BACON ON TOP

## Pasta Kids Mac and Cheese 

1. One ladle of alfredo sauce
2. One cup of nacho cheese sauce
3. **BE SURE TO MIX UP THE SAUCES**
4. ½ CUP OF MOZZARELLA CHEESE
5. ½ CUP OF CHEDDAR CHEESE

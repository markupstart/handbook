## CLASSIC ITALIAN 

**BOTTOM BUN:** ITALIAN DRESSING, 4 SALAMI, 2 HAM(FOLDED), 10 SLICES PEPPERONI, 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** ITALIAN DRESSING, ONION, TOMATO, 8 BANANA PEPPERS, 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE
 
## HAM AND CHEESE

**BOTTOM BUN:** 2 SLICES OF HAM (FOLDED LONG WAYS), 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** 2 SLICES OF HAM (FOLDED LONG WAYS), 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE

## CHICKEN BACON RANCH

**BOTTOM BUN:** RANCH, 3.5 OZ CHICKEN, 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** RANCH, 1 OZ BACON, 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE
 
## CHICKEN BACON BBQ

**BOTTOM BUN:** BBQ SAUCE, 3.5 OZ CHICKEN, 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** BBQ SAUCE, 1 OZ BACON, 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE
 
## TURKEY CLUB

**BOTTOM BUN:** MAYO, 2.5 OZ TURKEY, 2 SLICES OF CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** MAYO, 1 OZ BACON, 8 TOMATOES, 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE

## PHILLY CHEESESTEAK

1. LIGHTLY BUTTER  BOWL 
2. USE 2 OZ OF PHILLY MEAT
3. ADD IN 1 OZ ONIONS, 1OZ MUSHROOMS, 1OZ GREEN PEPPERS 
4. MICROWAVE FOR 1 ½ MIN

**BOTTOM BUN:** SPREAD ALL TOPPINGS, 2 SLICES OF CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE

## FRENCH DIP

1. 4 OZ ROAST BEEF
2. MICROWAVE FOR 1 MINUTE

**BOTTOM BUN:** 4 OZ ROAST BEEF, 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE

## MEATBALL MARINARA

**BOTTOM BUN:** 1/3 SCOOP OF MARINARA SAUCE, 17-20 MEATBALLS, PARM SHAKE, 2 SLICES OF CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** 1/3 SCOOP OF MARINARA, 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE

## BBQ PULLED PORK

**BOTTOM BUN:** BBQ SAUCE, 3 OZ PULLED PORK, 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** BBQ SAUCE, 2 HALF SLICES CHEESE

## VEGGIE

**BOTTOM BUN:** GARLIC BUTTER, MUSHROOM, ONION, GREEN PEPPER, 2 SLICES CHEESE

**TOP BUN:** GARLIC BUTTER, TOMATO, 8 BANANA PEPPERS,  2 HALF SLICES CHEESE
